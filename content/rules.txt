You have to put towers on the map.
To kill enemies before they reach the end of the path.
You will need to manage your money to install towers and improve them.
If 5 enemies cross the end of the road, you lose.
To lay towers you have to click on the holes in the grass or snow ....
Press Space for the pause menu, and echap to quit.
For the map you can make the path with . and B for the start, E for the End.
You put # everywhere around the path and T for the tower.
For example -->
    ##T####T#T#####
    #B.........................................E#
    ####T###T#T####
Tower --> 50 pieces.
Sell Tower --> 25 pieces.
